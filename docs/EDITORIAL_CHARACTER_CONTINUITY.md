# Editorial Character Continuity

## Scope

This document describes the prototype character continuity rules for the `#student-book-reference` lesson.

The current images are generated prototype assets. They are acceptable for visual direction review, but they are not final production photography.

## Anna

- Age: 29
- Appearance: adult learner, friendly expression, natural city style
- Hair: medium-length brown hair
- Clothing: smart casual top or jacket suitable for a language club
- Accessories: simple bag or notebook may appear
- Role: Beginner student meeting new people at ULC
- Relationship: meets Leo first; Maya joins the conversation
- Scene links: `anna-portrait`, `meeting-wide`, `listening-1`, `listening-2`, `speaking-1`

## Leo

- Age: 32
- Appearance: adult learner, calm professional look
- Hair: short dark hair
- Clothing: smart casual shirt or jacket
- Accessories: phone or notebook may appear in contact-detail scenes
- Role: new person at the language club; gives city and job information
- Relationship: meets Anna; later speaks with Anna and Maya
- Scene links: `leo-portrait`, `meeting-wide`, `listening-2`, `listening-4`, `listening-5`

## Maya

- Age: 27
- Appearance: adult teacher or host, warm and approachable
- Hair: shoulder-length hair
- Clothing: modern teacher / facilitator style
- Accessories: clipboard, tablet, or lesson notes may appear
- Role: ULC host/teacher who joins and supports the conversation
- Relationship: joins Anna and Leo after their first exchange
- Scene links: `maya-portrait`, `listening-3`, `listening-4`

## Shared Scenes

- Language club arrival: `club-wide`, `listening-1`
- First meeting: `meeting-wide`, `listening-2`
- Maya joins: `listening-3`
- City and job exchange: `listening-4`
- Contact exchange: `listening-5`
- Pair practice prompts: `speaking-1`, `speaking-2`, `speaking-3`
